About Toyin

The story behind the food and crafts

Meet Toyin

Hello! I'm Toyin, a passionate caterer and craftswoman based in Lagos, Nigeria. For over 5 years, I've been bringing joy to celebrations through my food and handmade crafts.

What started as a small weekend business has grown into a full-service kitchen and craft studio. I personally oversee every order, ensuring that each dish is perfectly seasoned and every bead strand is carefully crafted.

My mission is simple: to make your celebrations memorable with delicious food and beautiful crafts. Whether it's a birthday party, wedding, or corporate event, I put love into everything I make.
